Personalities!

Goofy, playful, portable, and loyal




Most corgis are friendly, outgoing, and playful, but at times, they can be protective and stubborn. Corgis are highly alert and can be quite the little barkers. There are only two breeds of corgis, which are both part of the herding group.

— Information from the American Kennel Club.





One fun fact about corgis is that they engage in the act of frapping, which is more commonly known as, the zoomies. You can learn more about this act here at the American Kennel Club. Another fun fact about corgis, is that Corgi means "dwarf dog". Or it might... It is difficult to determine the true origin of the name "Corgi".
