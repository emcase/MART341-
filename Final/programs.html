Workshops and Trainings

  • 200HR Teacher Training

    This training is for anyone who wishes to deepen their understanding of yoga and/or teach yoga to others. This training is a Yoga Alliance certified training that allows anyone to share the gift of yoga with others while mastering asana (poses) and pranayama (breathe) in your personal practice. In other words, use what you learn in this training and take it anywhere.

  • Vinyasa Flow

    Begin with a swift warm up and get ready to build heat from the inside out, get sweaty with it, connect the mind and body through breath and learn how to breathe through ANYTHING. Perfect for the day that you wake up with a crazy list of things to-do and need to focus on getting.stuff.done. Leave feeling balanced and energized.

  • Yin Yoga

    The practice of yin yoga offers you the opportunity to balance the active and external yang energy with the passive and internal yin energy. You will be guided through a series of floor-based poses (held 3-5 minutes) which targets the deeper connective tissue, including ligaments, joints and fascia. Using the support of props, active relaxation of muscles is encouraged, as you find your way into a gentle meditation.Perfect for the day that you want to sustain awesome energy or need to relax your busy mind! All levels welcomed.

  • SerenityFlow

    SerenityFlow is Serenity Yogas's signature format. This mind-body practice moves as a wave, starting with a deep breathing, a grounded warm up, transitioning into a flow of standing postures. Once familiar with the postures you'll flow, breath-to-movement, peaking at fun and challenging balancing postures. The wave will take you into a blissful and connected cool down. The intention of this practice is to remove blockages in the body and mind, opening you up to endless possibility. This class moves and is taught as a truly ALL levels class.
